Weekly Musical Theatre classes for children in Brackley, Warwick and Leamington Spa

Welcome to our vibrant Performing Arts School, where young imaginations flourish and creativity takes centre stage! We offer a range of weekly classes that cater to children aged 3+, providing a fun and enriching experience for every aspiring performer through singing, dancing and acting.

Adventurers: For our youngest stars aged 3-6, we host magical sessions every Thursday at All Saints CofE Junior School in Warwick and every Friday at Bracken Leas School in Brackley. From 4:30 PM to 5:30 PM, these little explorers climb aboard a time machine to journey through exciting new places. Each week, they dive into a new adventure—whether it’s the lush Amazon rainforest or the ancient Egyptian pyramids—through a delightful mix of singing, dancing, and acting.

Main Stages: For children aged 6 and up, our Main Stages class runs from 5:30 PM to 7:45 PM Thursdays at All Saints CofE Junior School in Warwick and Fridays at Bracken Leas School, Brackley. This class offers an in-depth experience with three specialised 45-minute sessions in singing, dancing, and acting each week. Our curriculum is designed around different thematic terms including Show Term, Skills Term, and Exploring Performing Arts Genres, ensuring that students continually grow and learn while enjoying diverse experiences.

At iSDA, we are passionate about fostering confidence, building self-esteem, and encouraging creativity. Our classes are designed to help children develop new skills and make new friends.
